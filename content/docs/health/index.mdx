#### General tips

- 8 hrs of sleep
- gym at least 4 times a week
  - lift heavy and make the muscles BURN!!
  - push till failure
  - 1g of protein per 1lb of body weight
  - creatine is GOATED
  - stick to a single program
- bulking
  - calorie surplus
    - eat more calories than you burn
  - dirty bulking
    - EVERYTHING I SEE I EAT!!!
    - faster weight gain but fat accumulation
  - clean bulking
    - quality over quantity
    - slower weight gain but little fat accumulation
- food
  - minimize junk food
  - wholefoods
  - meal prep
  - track macros
