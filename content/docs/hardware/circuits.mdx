## Series circuit

Components that come one after another

- in a series circuit, it does not matter where you place a resistor. For example, the resistor can come after or before an LED (connected to the anode or cathode)
  https://forum.arduino.cc/t/where-to-place-a-resistor/232935/7

## Parallel circuit

- components that run side by side
  - electrical current is split
